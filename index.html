Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

Nestled in the heart of Jeypore, Koraput, Andhra Hotel stands as a symbol of warm hospitality, rich traditions, and authentic culinary excellence. Inspired by the royal heritage of Jeypore and the vibrant cultural spirit of Koraput, our journey is rooted in a commitment to serving guests with genuine care and unforgettable experiences.

At Andhra Hotel, we honor this legacy by bringing together traditional flavors, exceptional service, and a welcoming atmosphere. Our recipes are inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Andhra Pradesh and Odisha, carefully crafted to offer guests an authentic taste of the region.
